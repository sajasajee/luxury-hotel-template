The Property

Built into the rock,
shaped by the light.

Aurelia Bay began as a single fisherman's villa in 1962. Three generations later, it remains family-run — expanded stone by stone, never past the point where it would stop feeling like somewhere to be quiet. No two suites share a floor plan; no two mornings share a view.
